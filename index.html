ADVENTURE SPIRIT, URBAN STYLE

The ADV350 stands out from the crowd. Its rugged design has adventure in its blood, thanks to long-travel suspension and block-pattern tyres, matched to a sporty engine that powers through every challenge with HSTC traction management. Fully equipped with large under seat storage area, adjustable screen, Smart Key and LCD dash – with the connectivity of Honda Smartphone Voice Control system – it’s a unique scooter big on performance, style and urban practicality

Fully Prepared for Adventure

The thrill of the outdoors mixed with sleek urban sophistication. Meet a tough crossover scooter built to conquer the highway, city block or unpaved road in equal measure. In serious style.

Negative LCD Display, HSVCs and Smart Key

The dash is easy to read and the ADV350 comes complete with Honda Smartphone Voice Control system, for phone calls and music. A Smart Key offers full operation – including the optional Smart Top Box – from your pocket.

Off-Road Style Wheels and Block Pattern Tyres Roll Smoothly

X-shaped, 6-spoke criss-cross pattern cast aluminium wheels are smooth rolling. They run off-road style block-pattern tyres for all-terrain versatility and traction.

Large Storage Compartment Ensures Flexible Convenience

You can fit two full-face helmets under the seat – perfect for when you arrive at your destination – and a built-in USB Type-C glovebox socket keeps a smartphone charging.

Long Travel Suspension and Extended Ground Clearance

There’s a full 145mm clearance underneath to clear obstacles, while the Up Side Down (USD) forks and rear shocks inspire handling confidence.

THE THRILL OF POSSIBILITIES

Confidence. That’s what the ADV350 gives you. It combines sporty engine power and robust, smooth-running chassis to cover terrain with ease. It’s good to stay connected while you ride and carry everything you need. So do just that, with HSVCs, via the Honda RoadSync app and plenty of practical storage room. Striking styling also marks out a difference – a good feeling when you park and, while the Smart Key in your pocket operates the ADV350, it also helps you find it in a crowd by flashing the lights. Your day might take you in, around and out of town but you don’t mind. You have an ADV350. You know what it can do.

SMART TOP BOX

Uses the ADV350’s Smart Key to unlock while you’re near, and lock when away. A colour-matched insert panel is an option, as is passenger backrest.

HEATED GRIPS

If you’re riding all year round, then heated grips are a must to keep hands warm in winter; requires separate attachment kit for fitting.
